At the beginning, Diana and Charlie were in the kitchen; Fiona and George were in the locker room; and Rachel and Wesley were in the classroom. Aaron and Yusuf were in the record room, and Ivy and Jack were in the courtyard. Diana was carrying the tablet, the blanket, the bottle cap, and the card in the kitchen. Fiona was carrying the map in the locker room. George was carrying the pillow in the locker room. Charlie was carrying the coin in the kitchen. Yusuf had the candle with them in the record room. The bottle was lying in the music room, and the notebook was lying in the garage.

First, George passed the pillow to Fiona in the locker room. Afterward, in the kitchen, Charlie gave the coin to Diana. After that, Diana handed the tablet to Charlie in the kitchen. Soon afterward, Fiona dropped the map in the locker room. Then, Diana set the coin down in the kitchen. Next, in the locker room, Fiona picked up the map. Then, Diana handed the blanket to Charlie in the kitchen. Before long, in the record room, Yusuf put the candle down. After that, Aaron picked up the candle in the record room. Jack moved from the courtyard to the locker room. Fiona set the map down in the locker room. Later, in the record room, Aaron put the candle down. Diana dropped the bottle cap in the kitchen. Still in the kitchen, Diana dropped the card. A little later, Diana picked up the coin while still in the kitchen.
